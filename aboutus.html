About Private Time

Welcome to Private Time, your number one source for all things ebook. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of reading material, with a focus on source, category, and convenience. Founded in 2020 by John Gardner, Private Time Online has come a long way from its beginnings in a dusty attic. When John Gardner first started out, his passion for reading and access to material drove him to open a little shop in his attic, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over world, and are thrilled to be a part of the ebook industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely, John Gardner, CEO
